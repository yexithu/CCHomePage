TCSOL/TESOL Dual Certifiicate Program

Students come to the program with a wide range of backgrounds but everyone shares boundless enthusiasm and passion for language teaching. During the TCSOL/TESOL Dual Certificate Program, I receive intensive training in the teaching of both Chinese and English from world-class team of professors. In class, we work collaboratively in teams to discuss grammatical questions and design communicative activities. After class, we share deep thoughts and new inspirations on discussion board online. Every time I submit my assignments and receive feedbacks I feel I am making steady progress. The program has become so much more than what I expected. I am thankful for this opportunity to learn educational linguistics and second language pedagogy of both Chinese and English in the summer dual program, which provides me a comparative perspective to view teaching in the two languages. With the solid foundation that the summer program provides, I am better equipped for my further graduate study in this field.
